# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Administrators, teachers, students, and guardians of MAN 2 Yogyakarta use this system from desktop and mobile browsers. Admins manage academic data and content quickly with low error tolerance. Public visitors consume school information, flyers, media, and videos in a clear and trusted format.

## Product Purpose
SIMANDAYA is a school management and information platform for MAN 2 Yogyakarta. It centralizes academic operations, personnel records, and public communication content. Success means users can complete tasks quickly, content is readable and consistent across devices, and key public media sections remain stable even when source images have mixed dimensions.

## Brand Personality
Trustworthy, practical, and structured. The interface should feel institutional but modern, direct, and easy to scan. It should communicate reliability over decoration.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic startup SaaS visuals, over-animated glass effects, and decorative gradients that reduce readability. Avoid inconsistent card sizing and unpredictable media crops that make school content look broken or informal.

## Design Principles
1. Prioritize operational clarity over visual novelty.
2. Keep public content modules consistent in size and rhythm.
3. Separate primary hero communication from secondary media galleries.
4. Make error prevention explicit in forms and content management flows.
5. Preserve readability and accessibility on both desktop and mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard accessibility for interactive controls. Respect reduced motion preferences, keep clear focus states, and avoid color-only meaning for status feedback.
