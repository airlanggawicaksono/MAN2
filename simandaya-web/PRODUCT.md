# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary users are administrative staff at MAN 2 Yogyakarta who manage data, content, attendance, and school operations. Secondary users are public visitors who browse institutional information, services, and announcements. Admin tasks are frequent and workflow-heavy, while public visits are information-focused and lightweight.

## Product Purpose
Simandaya provides a single web surface for school administration and public information services. It helps staff complete operational tasks accurately and quickly, while giving visitors clear access to educational services and school resources. Success means fewer workflow frictions for staff, consistent navigation across modules, and clear, trustworthy public-facing information.

## Brand Personality
Formal, calm, trustworthy. The interface should feel educational, orderly, and dependable. Tone should be direct and respectful, with clear labels and low visual noise.

## Anti-references
Avoid playful consumer-app styling, loud marketing aesthetics, and novelty-heavy dashboard visuals. Avoid excessive motion, decorative effects, and inconsistent component treatments between pages. Avoid visual choices that make the product feel like a social media feed or entertainment site.

## Design Principles
1. Operational clarity first: Admin workflows should prioritize fast scanning, predictable structure, and direct actions.
2. One interface language: Navigation, forms, tables, and feedback states should look and behave consistently across modules.
3. Calm educational tone: Visual decisions should support institutional trust and reduce cognitive load.
4. Purposeful hierarchy: Typography, spacing, and color should guide tasks without visual clutter.
5. Low-motion feedback: Motion should communicate state changes only, with short and subtle transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction clarity. Support reduced-motion preferences by minimizing non-essential animation and avoiding motion-heavy transitions. Ensure focus states are visible, form controls are clearly labeled, and text remains legible across desktop and mobile surfaces.
